Hippopotamus

The name Hippopotamus comes from the Ancient Greek 'river horse.

  • Lifespan: 40-50 years
  • Diet: Herbivore
  • Avrage Mass: 1,500Kg
  • Speed: 30 km/h (On Land, Running)

The common hippopotamus inhabits rivers, lakes and mangrove swamps, where territorial bulls preside over a stretch of river and groups of five to thirty females and young.
